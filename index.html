2024 — 2025

Founded AI design tool

Ghost

Ghost started as a simple idea to automate design using AI. What if you could describe a presentation and have it just exist? Under the hood it's generating real HTML slides that get rendered via a custom browser engine, but to the user it feels like magic. It grew to 1,300 users and landed in the Top 10 on Product Hunt, competing against launches from Google and Microsoft.
